An inno­vation in music practice is on its way.

In 2022, we submitted Virtuo - a music practice app built on Anvil and Replit - to the Young ICT Explorers Competition. Our feedback told us one key thing: the idea was awesome. But the prototype we made was incomplete and not functional.

So this year, we're finishing what we started. And, with a new development team and new ideas, we're tackling it from scratch*.
